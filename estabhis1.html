Estabs Brief History

The Office of Establishments and Training was created in 1976. However, it keeps changing in name. At a time, it was called Office of Establishments and Management Services while at another time, it was called Office of Establishments and Service Matters

The Office is an arm of the Office of the Head of Service, headed by the Permanent Secretary with three main departments viz. Establishments, Training & Manpower Development and Industrial & Labour Relations.

The Office of Establishments and Training is a core human resource management office, saddled with the responsibilities of human capital development, career management for centrally deployed officers, Institutional/ organizational policy reform, Coordinating Secretariat of Standing Committee on Labour matters and discipline of erring officers.

The Office also represents the State at National Council on Establishments meetings.

Vision:  To achieve a better public service , better institutions, better governance and better Ondo State people.
Mission:  To develop and coordinate ideas that would enhance productivity, improve processes, and ensure effective service delivery in Ondo state
