Key Goal

" Blueprint to Progress"

 The Blueprint is anchored on five(5) Cardinal Programmes , which are popularly known as platforms for change and it's encaspsulated in the acronym - JMPPR.

J- Job Creation through Agriculture , Entreprenuership and Industrilization

M- Massive Infrastucture Development and Maintenance

P-Provision of Functional Education and Technological Growth

P-Provision of Accessible and Qualitative Health Care and Social Service Delivery

R-Rural Development and Community Extension Services

Vision:  To achieve a better public service , better institutions, better governance and better Ondo State people.
Mission:  To develop and coordinate ideas that would enhance productivity, improve processes, and ensure effective service delivery in Ondo state
